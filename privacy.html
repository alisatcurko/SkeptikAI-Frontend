Privacy Policy for SkeptikAI

Effective Date: December 3, 2024

Overview

SkeptikAI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our scam detection service.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Messages for Analysis: When you submit text or screenshots for scam detection, we temporarily process this content to provide our service.
  • Email Address (Optional): If you sign up for beta updates or Pro tier notifications, we collect your email address.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: We may collect basic analytics about how the service is used (page views, feature usage).
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information for security and service improvement.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide scam detection analysis
  • Improve our AI detection algorithms
  • Send beta updates or Pro tier launch notifications (if you opted in)
  • Prevent abuse and ensure service security

Data Storage and Security

  • No Permanent Storage: We do NOT permanently store the messages or screenshots you submit for analysis. Content is processed in real-time and discarded after analysis.
  • Security Measures: We use industry-standard security practices to protect any data we temporarily process.
  • No Selling: We will NEVER sell your data to third parties.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Analytics (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Email notifications (e.g., email service providers)
  • Payment processing (e.g., Stripe - when Pro tier launches)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your email address from our notification list
  • Opt out of analytics tracking
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

Beta Program

During our beta period:

  • The service is provided free of charge
  • We may collect feedback to improve the product
  • Features and policies may change as we develop the product
